Counterfactual section
  • Baseline — score of the weakest trial (the naive starting point)
  • Winner — score of the best trial
  • Δ — winner minus baseline. Positive = improvement
  • Cost Δ per 1k requests — how much more/less the winner costs to run at scale
  • p95 latency Δ — how much slower/faster the winner is at the 95th percentile
